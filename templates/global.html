Global View — Reconstructed from All Fragments

This page simulates the central office in Algiers querying across all regional databases. North Agency and East Agency each store only their regional data — this view merges them all, exactly as the lab's All_Trips global view did in Oracle.

-- Oracle equivalent (from lab):

  SELECT * FROM trips_north  -- North Agency DB
  UNION
  SELECT * FROM trips_east;   -- East Agency DB
